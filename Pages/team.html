Meet Our Team

The whole IITGN community is a part of Nyasa team in one way or another. We are privileged to have volunteers from students, staff, faculty of IITGN and even of other institutions for all of our events. Though never falling short of volunteers on any event, Nyasa still needs people who are dedicated to commit full time for the efficient working of the initiative.
